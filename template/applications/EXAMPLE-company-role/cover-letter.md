# Cover letter — Example Corp, Senior Widget Engineer

Dear Hiring Team,

Opening line on why this specific role and company caught your eye. Keep it
genuine; if you have a real connection to what they do, say so.

A short paragraph mapping two or three of your concrete accomplishments to what
the posting actually asks for. Pull these from job.md so they line up with how
the resume is positioned.

A closing line on what you would bring and an invitation to talk.

Best,
Your Name
